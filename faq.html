What is this website for?

Don't you want to pay all the channels to be able to watch football?

Tired of always looking for match summaries on the internet?

This website is made for you! You will be able to find all summaries as well as highlights in one place!

What do I have to do?

Nothing! You can enjoy this website without doing anything.

If you want a personalized page, you can register and thus see the summaries of the matches of your favorite team

How can I see videos of a particular team?

If you go to the "Highlights" page, it is possible to filter by championship and / or by team in order to see only the videos that interest you
